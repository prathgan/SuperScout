FTC SuperScout

Welcome to the Horace Mann Basement Lions scouting app, FTC SuperScout. This app lets you fill out logs of teams you interact with during scouting sessions and througout the day, and automatically stores them to an online database! In order to use the app, you must create an account in order to maintain reliabiltiy of information sources. To get started, hit the START button below.

*In order to see the team notes and matches logged on this app, please visit superscoutportal.github.io on the web
